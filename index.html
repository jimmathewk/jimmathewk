World's most illuminating experiences

In celebration of the United Nations' Year of Light in 2015, travel authority Lonely Planet has compiled a list of the 10 best light attractions in the world. First on this list, is the unmistakeable and unpredictable "Northern Lights" observed from Abisko, Sweden. Abisko's Aurora Skystation is the perfect place to watch the heavenly glow across the perfectly black sky, held polution free by the mountains surronding the Skystation. The list boasts of many more such brilliantly bright, bedazzling places that you should definitely have a look at.
